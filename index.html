Another Great YWC Event!

Are you over 30 years old and ready to race? If so, you are qualified to race in the decades long Winter Club tradition that is Ancient Jocks! Participants will take place in a timed slalom race with a beer stop in the middle. Chug that beer as fast as you can and race the course to the finish. Awards will be given to the top 3 fastest racers in the following age categories:

  • 30-39 Women/Men
  • 40-49 Women/Men
  • 50-59 Women/Men
  • 60+ Women/Men
